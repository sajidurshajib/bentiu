About Us

Bentiu University College of Petroleum (BUCP) is an academic public institution of higher education, founded in 2010. The University College of Petroleum was originally known as Bentiu University. It is one of the four new Public Universities (Project universities) decreed by the former President of Sudan, Omar Hassan Al Bashir with the purpose of making unity attractive as South Sudanese were scheduled to conduct Referendum for the Southern part of the country in st 2011 (Resolution of the 1 Session of the NCHE, No3.,2012,p.3).
BUCP is mandated to carry out professional development training and produce experts, among others in field of Oil and Gas industry, Environmental Studies, which include water management, Science and Technology, Innovation and Entrepreneurship.
