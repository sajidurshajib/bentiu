About Us

Bentiu University Collage of Petroleum (BUCP) is an academic institution of higher education, founded in 2010. The University Collage of Petroleum was originally known as Bentiu University, it was one of the four Public Universities (project universities ) decreed by the former president of Sudan Omar Hassan Al Bashir with the purpose of making unity attractive as South Sudanese were scheduled to conduct Referendum for the southern part of the country in 2011 (Resolution of the 1st Session of the NCHE, No3.,2012,p.3).
