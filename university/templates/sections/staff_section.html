Staff

BUCP has 12 full time academic and administrative staff and 9 part time staff, most of whom were reappointed in 2022. The University College is expected to have it first intake in 2024, after the teaching activities were interfered with by the 2013 crisis in the country. Originally, the college adopted a Kenyan curriculum which is now under review with the of Specialized Sub-Committee of National Council Higher Education of the Republic of South Sudan.
