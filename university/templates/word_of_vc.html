A Word from the Vice Chancellor



I am privilege to lead Bentiu University of Petroleum (BUP) during it early development. I am proud of the trust bestowed upon me and my team who are fully committed to ensure that BUP stand with its own feet and become operational. This is an important mission, because we want our sons and daughters of South Sudan and the greater Unity State in particular, to enjoy the fruits of this educational facility for many years to come.

I personally believe that BUP will contribute significantly towards nation building, peaceful co- existent and harmony of all the beautiful communities of South Sudan and to enhance development for sharing future.

I therefore take this opportunity to appeal to all South Sudanese to join us and contribute in any way they could within their capabilities to the growth of this university.

Thank you very much for your support. For God and my country!

Prof. Melha Rout Biel

Juba, May 2024

Vice Chancellor (Principal)
